The Leela Kovalam,A

Raviz Hotel

Perched majestically on a cliffside overlooking the Arabian Sea, The Leela Kovalam, A Raviz Hotel, is India’s only clifftop 5-star resort - a timeless retreat designed by Charles Correa. Spread across 67 acres, it includes the historic Halcyon Castle, once the royal summer residence of Travancore’s kings.

Whitewashed walls, red-tiled roofs, and panoramic sundecks frame sweeping ocean vistas, while the sea breeze and rhythmic waves create a symphony of serenity. Explore local markets, dive into coastal adventures, and dine on the freshest seafood, straight from the day’s catch.

Wander through Aaramam, our sprawling 5-acre organic vegetable garden, or unwind with Ayurvedic rituals at the spa. More than a stay, this is a royal embrace of Kerala’s coastal soul.
